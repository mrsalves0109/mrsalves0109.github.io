How to use your Metrocard

Oh, the place's you can go...using your Metrocard! MetroCards are easy to use.
Just “swipe” your card through the subway turnstile card reader.
Or “dip” your card in the bus farebox. See video below.








Check out MTA.com for help



When swiping your Metrocard, if the following messege appears "Please Swipe Again",
Swipe again at the same turnstile until you see a messege with your current balance.
Do not move to another turnstile and swipe, you might be charged another fare.
